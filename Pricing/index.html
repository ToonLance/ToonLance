Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our animation services and process.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most 15-second animations take 1-3 weeks from concept to completion. We provide specific timelines for each project during the consultation phase.

Do you work with international clients?

Yes, we work with clients worldwide. All communication is conducted in English, and we're experienced in collaborating across different time zones.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in all standard video formats (MP4, MOV, AVI) at your preferred resolution. Source files (PSD, AI, etc.) are included with Premium packages.

Can I request custom pricing?

Absolutely! For larger projects or specific requirements, we offer custom quotes. Contact us with your project details for a personalized estimate.
