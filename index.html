Our Creative Process

We follow a straightforward method to create 2D animations that work for you.

1

Concept Sketch

We start by discussing your ideas and sketching out the basics to align on direction.

  • Initial client consultation
  • Creative brainstorming
  • Project scope definition
  • Reference material collection
2

Storyboard

We lay out the scenes in detail to visualize how the story will unfold.

  • Script development
  • Scene-by-scene visualization
  • Timing and pacing planning
  • Client review and approval
3

Rough Animation

We build the rough movements and elements to get a sense of the flow.

  • Character design & development
  • Environment and prop creation
  • Color palette selection
  • Style frame development
4

Clean Up

We refine everything for smooth, polished animation ready for delivery.

  • Keyframe animation
  • Character rigging and posing
  • Motion refinement
  • Camera animation
